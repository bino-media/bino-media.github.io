BINO CHILD SAFETY STANDARDS

Bino is committed to providing a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all users. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM), in compliance with applicable laws and platform requirements, including Google Play policies.

Bino strictly prohibits any activity, content, or behavior that endangers, exploits, or harms children.

1. Scope and Commitment

These Child Safety Standards apply to all users and all forms of content on the Bino platform, including but not limited to text, images, videos, comments, messages, and user profiles.

Bino strictly prohibits any activity that endangers, exploits, or harms children.

2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Bino:

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE)
  • Uploading, sharing, storing, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming behavior, including building relationships with minors for exploitative purposes
  • Sexualized interactions involving minors in any form
  • Harassment, coercion, or threats directed at minors
  • Any attempt to circumvent detection or moderation systems

Violations of these rules are considered severe and will result in immediate enforcement actions.

3. Content Moderation and Detection

Bino employs a multi-layered moderation approach to prevent and detect harmful content:

  • Automated systems to identify potentially abusive or high-risk content
  • Human moderation to review flagged or reported content
  • User reporting mechanisms to surface violations quickly

We continuously improve our detection and enforcement capabilities to enhance child safety.

4. User Reporting and In-App Safety Tools

Bino provides accessible in-app tools that allow users to report content or behavior that may violate these standards.

Users can:

  • Report posts, comments, or user accounts
  • Select specific violation categories, including child safety concerns
  • Submit reports directly within the app interface

All reports are reviewed in a timely manner by our moderation team.

5. Enforcement and Penalties

Bino enforces its policies through a range of actions, depending on the severity of the violation:

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Temporary restriction of account functionality
  • Suspension or permanent termination of accounts
  • Blocking access to platform features
  • Escalation to relevant authorities when required

Repeat or severe violations will result in permanent account bans.

6. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Bino complies with all applicable child protection laws and regulations. Where required, we will:

  • Report illegal content or behavior to relevant authorities
  • Cooperate with law enforcement investigations
  • Preserve necessary data in accordance with legal obligations

7. Child Safety by Design

Bino integrates safety considerations into product design and operations, including:

  • Providing clear reporting mechanisms
  • Limiting exposure to harmful content
  • Reviewing high-risk content categories
  • Continuously improving safety policies and enforcement workflows

8. User Eligibility

Bino is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by local laws). Users must meet the minimum age requirement to use the platform.

9. Privacy and Data Protection

Bino respects user privacy and processes personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Access to user-generated content is strictly limited to purposes such as:

  • Content moderation
  • Safety enforcement
  • Legal compliance

For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

10. Contact and Escalation

For any concerns related to child safety, CSAE, or CSAM, users and authorities may contact us at:

Email: contact@binomedia.vn

We are committed to responding promptly and taking appropriate action.

11. Policy Updates

Bino may update these Child Safety Standards from time to time to reflect legal, regulatory, or operational changes. Updates will be published on this page with the revised effective date.

Bino remains committed to maintaining a safe digital environment and continuously strengthening protections for children across our platform.
