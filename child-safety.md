> BINO strictly prohibits any content, conduct, or interaction that exploits, sexualizes, abuses, harms, or endangers children. Confirmed or suspected violations may result in immediate removal, account suspension or termination, and referral to the appropriate authorities where required by law.

BINO is committed to maintaining a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all users. We adopt a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation, including child sexual abuse material, grooming, predatory behavior, and any conduct that endangers minors.

## 1. Purpose and Scope

These Child Safety Standards set out BINO's rules, safeguards, and enforcement practices relating to child safety on the platform. They apply to all users and to all forms of activity and content on BINO, including without limitation text, images, videos, comments, messages, user profiles, posts, and other user-generated or shared materials.

BINO is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation and to maintaining standards designed to detect, deter, review, and address content or conduct that may place minors at risk.

## 2. Definitions

- **Child / Minor** — A person under the age of 18, or any higher age threshold established by applicable law.
- **CSAE** — Child sexual abuse and exploitation, including conduct, solicitation, facilitation, grooming, coercion, or exploitation involving minors.
- **CSAM** — Any visual depiction, representation, or material involving the sexual abuse, exploitation, or sexualized depiction of a minor, as defined by applicable law.

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on BINO:

- Any content that depicts, promotes, glorifies, requests, or facilitates CSAE or CSAM
- Uploading, sharing, storing, transmitting, or distributing CSAM in any form
- Grooming, predatory behavior, or attempts to build exploitative relationships with minors
- Sexualized comments, interactions, threats, coercion, extortion, or harassment involving minors
- Attempts to exploit, manipulate, or endanger minors through direct or indirect communication
- Any effort to evade, circumvent, interfere with, or undermine safety, moderation, or detection systems

Violations of these standards are treated as severe policy breaches and may result in immediate enforcement action without prior notice.

## 4. Reporting and In-App Safety Tools

BINO provides in-app reporting tools that enable users to report content, accounts, or behavior that may violate these standards or otherwise raise child safety concerns.

Users may, where available within the app:

- Report posts, comments, messages, profiles, or accounts
- Select report categories related to child safety or harmful conduct
- Submit reports directly through the app interface for moderation review

Reports are reviewed by BINO's moderation processes in a timely manner and may be escalated for enhanced review depending on severity, risk indicators, or legal obligations.

## 5. Moderation and Detection

BINO uses a layered safety approach intended to prevent, identify, and address harmful conduct. This may include:

- Automated systems and signals to identify potentially abusive or high-risk content or behavior
- Human review of flagged, reported, or otherwise escalated material
- Internal safety workflows to support investigation, triage, and enforcement

BINO may revise and improve its moderation, trust, and safety systems from time to time in order to strengthen child protection measures.

## 6. Enforcement Measures

Where BINO determines, in its sole discretion and subject to applicable law, that a user, account, or piece of content may violate these standards, BINO may take one or more of the following actions:

- Remove or restrict access to violating content
- Temporarily restrict account functionality or feature access
- Suspend or permanently terminate accounts
- Block interactions or access to specific platform services
- Preserve relevant records where required for safety or legal purposes
- Escalate the matter to the appropriate authorities where required or permitted by law

Repeated, egregious, or high-risk violations may result in permanent account removal.

## 7. Legal Compliance and Cooperation

BINO complies with applicable child protection, safety, and reporting laws and regulations. Where required by law, BINO may report confirmed or reasonably suspected illegal material or behavior to the appropriate regional or national authorities.

BINO may also, where legally required or permitted:

- Cooperate with law enforcement or competent authorities
- Respond to lawful requests relating to investigations
- Preserve or disclose relevant information in accordance with applicable law

## 8. Age Eligibility

BINO is not intended for children under the age of 13, or any higher minimum age required under applicable local law. Users are responsible for complying with applicable age requirements to access and use the platform.

## 9. Privacy and Data Handling

BINO respects user privacy and processes personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws and its published privacy documentation.

Access to user-generated content and account-related information may be limited to authorized purposes such as:

- Content moderation and trust & safety review
- Enforcement of platform policies and user protections
- Legal compliance, record preservation, and lawful reporting obligations

For more information, please refer to BINO's [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) and related legal documents, where applicable.

## 10. Contact Information

For concerns relating to child safety, CSAE, CSAM, harmful conduct involving minors, or compliance with these standards, users, guardians, regulators, and competent authorities may contact BINO at:

**Email:** [contact@binomedia.vn](mailto:contact@binomedia.vn)

BINO may update this contact point from time to time. Please use the most current contact information published on this page.

## 11. Policy Updates

BINO may amend, revise, or update these Child Safety Standards at any time to reflect changes in law, regulation, platform requirements, operational practices, or safety controls.

Any updates will be published on this page together with the revised effective date. Continued use of the platform after an update may be subject to the updated standards, to the extent permitted by applicable law.
